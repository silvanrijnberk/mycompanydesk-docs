# Business Page

Create a public profile for your business that customers can visit to learn about your services and request quotes.

## Overview

Your business page is a public web page that showcases:

- Your company information
- Services you offer
- Contact details
- Social media links
- A quote request form

## Setting up your business page

1. Go to **Company** settings
2. Fill in your **business profile** section:
   - **Description** — A brief overview of your business
   - **Services** — List the services you offer
   - **Website** — Your company website URL
   - **Social media** — Links to your social profiles
3. Your business page is automatically available at a unique URL

## Business page URL

Your page is accessible at:

```
https://app.mycompanydesk.com/portal/{your-company-slug}
```

Share this link on your website, social media, or business cards.

## Quote request form

Visitors to your business page can submit a quote request:

1. They fill in their **name**, **email**, and **service description**
2. The request is submitted
3. You receive a notification
4. The request appears in **Quotes > Requests**

### Rate limiting

To prevent spam, quote requests are limited to 1 per 15 minutes per IP address.

## Managing requests

See [Quotes — Quote Requests](/features/quotes#quote-requests) for details on managing incoming requests.

## Branding

Your business page uses your company branding:

- Company logo
- Accent color
- Company name and details

## Tips

- Keep your services list up to date
- Add a link to your business page on your website
- Respond to quote requests promptly for better conversion
- Use the quote request workflow to track leads
