# Feedback

Share your thoughts, report bugs, or request features directly from within MyCompanyDesk.

## Submitting feedback

1. Click your **profile menu** in the sidebar (or mobile menu)
2. Select **Feedback**
3. Choose a **category**:
   - **General** — General comments or questions
   - **Bug** — Report something that isn't working correctly
   - **Feature** — Suggest a new feature or improvement
   - **Usability** — Share feedback about the user experience
4. Optionally rate your experience (1-5 stars)
5. Write your **message** describing the feedback
6. Click **Submit**

After submitting, you'll see a confirmation screen. Your feedback is sent to the MyCompanyDesk team for review.

## What happens next

The team reviews all feedback submissions:

- **Bug reports** are prioritized and investigated
- **Feature requests** are considered for the product roadmap
- **Usability feedback** helps improve the interface and workflows

## Tips

- Be as specific as possible — include what you were doing and what you expected to happen
- For bugs, mention the page where the issue occurred (this is captured automatically)
- You can submit feedback from any page in the app
