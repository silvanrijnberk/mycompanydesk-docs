To delete your account:
1. Go to My Account → Security
2. Export your data first from My Account → Data if you need a backup
3. In the Danger Zone, click "Delete"
4. Confirm the deletion - this action is permanent

Important: Account deletion removes your access permanently. Export everything you need before proceeding.
