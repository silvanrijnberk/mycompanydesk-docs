To upload or replace a receipt on an expense:
1. Open an existing expense, or save a new expense first.
2. In the quick-view drawer, click the paperclip icon; or on the expense detail page, use the **Upload Receipt** action.
3. Select the image or PDF file you want to attach.
4. Wait for the upload to finish and confirm it appears on the expense.

Tip: If you are still on the new-expense form, save the expense first. Once the expense is saved, you can attach or replace its receipt from the quick-view drawer or the detail page.
